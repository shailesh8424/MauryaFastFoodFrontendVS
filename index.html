Welcome to Maurya Fast Food! Located in the heart of Borivali West, Mumbai – Maurya Fast Food is your go-to spot for fresh, delicious, and affordable Indian snacks.

  • Our popular dishes include Vada Pav, Samosa Pav, Samosa, Cutlets, Bread Pakora, Palak Pakoda, and Jalebi.
  • Every item is made with love, hygiene, and authentic flavors.
  • We are known for quick service, tasty food, and a clean environment.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or craving something crispy and hot, Maurya Fast Food is always ready to serve you.
